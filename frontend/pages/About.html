About The Project

SmartHub was designed to provide a secure, affordable, and modular platform for users who value their privacy.

Why SmartHub

Privacy

In today's world, privacy is a luxury. SmartHub is designed to give users an affordable smart home option without trading off their privacy. SmartHub will act as a secure hub for all of personal Data.

Control

SmartHub uses a user-centric design, allowing users to contorl their smart home devices from a single device.

Expandability

Our modular system allows users to add features that matter to them and will encorporate a simple plug and play system for futer expansion.
